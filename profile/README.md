## 📋 Overview

<a href="#tradingView">TradingView</a> is the world's most widely used financial charting and market analysis platform — providing professional-grade technical analysis tools, real-time market data across stocks, forex, cryptocurrencies, futures, and indices, and a global trading community on Mac in a native desktop application.

The <a href="#tradingView">professional charting engine</a> renders financial charts with the speed and rendering quality that active market analysis requires — candlestick, bar, line, area, Heikin Ashi, Renko, and many other chart types with smooth real-time data updates across multiple markets simultaneously. The charting engine handles the data density and update frequency of live market data without the lag and rendering issues that browser-based charting produces when monitoring multiple instruments in parallel. The <a href="#tradingView">technical indicators library</a> includes over 100 built-in technical analysis indicators — moving averages, RSI, MACD, Bollinger Bands, Fibonacci tools, volume indicators, momentum oscillators, and dozens of others — with configurable parameters and the ability to stack multiple indicators on the same chart for complex analytical setups.

The <a href="#tradingView">Pine Script custom indicator language</a> enables creating custom technical indicators and strategy scripts — writing indicator logic in TradingView's purpose-built scripting language and publishing them to the community or keeping them private. The large Pine Script community has published thousands of custom indicators covering specialized technical analysis approaches, trading system implementations, and market analysis tools that supplement the built-in indicator library. The <a href="#tradingView">drawing tools</a> add manual chart markup — trend lines, channels, Fibonacci retracements, geometric shapes, text annotations, and other drawing elements — for the chart analysis workflow that technical traders use to identify support and resistance levels, chart patterns, and price targets.</p>

<a href="#tradingView">Multi-chart layouts</a> display several instruments or timeframes simultaneously on the same screen — the multi-monitor equivalent within a single window, allowing simultaneous analysis of related instruments, multiple timeframes of the same instrument, or a watchlist of charts reviewed in parallel. <a href="#tradingView">Market screener</a> filters the full universe of stocks, forex pairs, or crypto assets by technical and fundamental criteria — finding instruments that meet specific technical conditions (RSI below 30, price crossing above 200-day MA) across thousands of instruments simultaneously.

<a href="#tradingView">Price alerts</a> trigger notifications when price reaches a specified level, when a technical indicator crosses a threshold, or when other market conditions occur — keeping traders informed of market developments without continuous chart monitoring. <a href="#tradingView">Social trading community</a> shares chart analysis, trade ideas, and market commentary from millions of traders — published ideas with annotated charts represent the collective technical analysis of the global TradingView community on any instrument.</p>

## 💬 FAQ

<details>
<summary>Is TradingView free?</summary>

Yes. A free tier provides substantial charting capability. Paid tiers add more simultaneous indicators, faster data refresh, more saved layouts, and other professional features.

</details>

<details>
<summary>Does TradingView have a desktop app for Mac?</summary>

Yes. A native Mac desktop application is available alongside the browser-based version.

</details>

<details>
<summary>What markets does TradingView cover?</summary>

Stocks, forex, cryptocurrencies, futures, indices, bonds, and other financial instruments across major global exchanges.

</details>

<details>
<summary>What is Pine Script?</summary>

Pine Script is TradingView's custom scripting language for creating technical indicators and backtestable trading strategies. Thousands of community-published scripts are available.

</details>

<details>
<summary>Can TradingView connect to a broker for trading?</summary>

Yes. TradingView integrates with compatible brokers for executing trades directly from the chart interface.

</details>

<details>
<summary>Does TradingView have price alerts?</summary>

Yes. Alerts trigger on price levels, indicator thresholds, and other technical conditions with notifications delivered to the desktop app and mobile.

</details>

<details>
<summary>What is the Market Screener?</summary>

The Screener filters the full universe of stocks, forex, or crypto by technical and fundamental criteria to find instruments meeting specific conditions.

</details>

<details>
<summary>Does TradingView sync across devices?</summary>

Yes. Charts, watchlists, indicators, and settings sync between the desktop app and other devices signed into the same account.

</details>

<details>
<summary>Can I share my chart analysis on TradingView?</summary>

Yes. Chart analyses publish to the TradingView community as Ideas with annotated charts visible to other users.

</details>
